FAQ

Subscription Maun Meeting Minutes service
  • Q. What kinds of services available for 99,000 KRW monthly plan?
    A. AI API, AI meeting log, AI Human image production, and AI customer call center service are available.
  • Q. Does the plan automatically ends after the first month free trial?
    A. If you did not register your payment information, it will be ended automatically after the trial period.
  • Q. What should I do if I want a subscription after the termination of a service?
    A. You can register or update your payment information as like as following order : maum.ai log-in > credit card register at “Payment information”
  • Q. Does annual payment available instead of monthly charge?
    A. Annual subscription is available. If the annual subscription fee is paid with the wire transfer(not by the credit card), annual access plan is available.
  • Q. Does AVA service is also included to 99,000 KRW plan?
    A. AVA is not included to the plan
  • Q. If the subscription is cancelled before the next payment date, does it still accessable till the due payment date?
    A. If the plan is already paid, the account is available until the next payment date.
  • Q. Does the service can be purchased with a corporate credit card?
    A. You can register a corporate card to your payment information
  • Q. Does maum.ai provides payment receipt?
    A. Payment receipt can be confirmed with the following procedure : log-in to maum.ai > click receipt button next to a purchased plan at the payment information page
  • Q. How much I can use Cloud meeting log with 99,000 KRW plan for a month? If I cannot use them all, does it transfer to the following month?
    A. 100 hours are available for a month, and it does not transfer the left over hours to the following month
  • Q. Does 99,000 KRW monthly plan provides speaker separation function for Maum Meeting Minutes?
    A. 99,000 KRW plan is a cloud-type meeting log which provides real-time recording and can upload voice and image/video files. Speaker separation function can be applied to On-Device meeting log service.
  • Q. Is monthly installment plan available for On-Device meeting log service?
    A. Installment can be made with Kookmin card all-lease. Contact CS for details.
  • Q. As On-Device service is made with 10 channels, however, if we only need limited numbers of channels with small participants (under 5 channels), will there be any difference to the plan price?
    A. The service is composited with 10 channels, however, please contact us for specific details through email.
  • Q. What is the price for On-Site service? What’s the difference comparing to On-Device service?
    A. The service is provided with the same mobile device for On-Device. You can use more than 10 channels and customization will be applied so that the system has better capability to understand and recognize sentences to meet a company requirements.
